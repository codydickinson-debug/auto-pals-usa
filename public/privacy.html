Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 11, 2026

Auto Pals USA ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use our website at autopalsusa.com, what we do with it, and what choices you have. By using our site or submitting a request, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

When you submit our vehicle request form, we collect the information you provide, including:

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Mailing address, city, state, and ZIP code
  • Vehicle preferences (make, model, year range, budget, features)
  • Any notes or additional context you provide

We also automatically receive limited technical data when you visit the site (browser type, IP address, page views) through our hosting provider's server logs. We do not use cookies for advertising or third-party tracking.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Source vehicles that match your request through dealer-only auctions
  • Contact you via email, phone, or SMS to coordinate the search and the resulting transaction
  • Send service-related notifications (booking confirmations, deposit reminders, portal messages, contract notices)
  • Maintain records of our work for you, including signed agreements and payment references

3. SMS / Text Message Communications

SMS opt-in & consent By providing your phone number on our request form, you consent to receive SMS messages from Auto Pals USA related to your vehicle search. The form displays this disclosure directly beneath the phone-number field at the point of collection.

What you'll receive: a one-time confirmation with a link to book an intro call; up to two reminders if you don't book the call within 48 hours; a notification when a staff member messages you in your client portal; and a notification when your service contract is ready to sign.

Frequency: typically 4–8 messages over the lifecycle of a single vehicle search; message frequency varies.

Cost: message and data rates may apply from your wireless carrier.

How to stop messages: reply STOP to any text from us and we will not send you further SMS. Reply HELP for help, or contact us at info@autopalsusa.com.

Your phone number is not shared with third parties or affiliates for their own marketing purposes. Your phone information and consent will not be shared with third parties for marketing, promotional, or any other purposes.

4. How We Share Information

We share information only with service providers we use to operate the business, and only as needed to deliver the services you requested:

  • Twilio — for sending SMS notifications
  • SendGrid — for sending email notifications
  • Supabase — for storing your request, messages, and account data
  • Google Calendar / Google Workspace — for scheduling intro calls
  • Vercel — for hosting the website

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information. We do not share your data with advertisers or marketing networks.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as needed to provide our services and to keep records required for tax, legal, and accounting purposes. You can request deletion of your information at any time by emailing info@autopalsusa.com, subject to any record-keeping requirements imposed by law.

6. Security

We use industry-standard practices to protect your information (encrypted transport, access controls on our admin tools, encrypted credentials with our service providers). No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure; if you have specific security concerns please contact us.

7. Children

Our services are not directed to anyone under the age of 18, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Your Rights

You may:

  • Access or correct the information we have about you
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to legal record-keeping)
  • Opt out of SMS messages at any time (reply STOP)
  • Opt out of email communications by replying to any of our emails and asking us to unsubscribe

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Material changes will be highlighted on the website.
